What this is

Not a campground. A movement with a coffee shop.

The idea

We’re taking an old school on the lake and the RV park around it and turning them into one destination — a place where families camp, kids play, locals gather, and businesses are built. The school is the heart; everything else grows around it.

Why it matters

This is Social Impact Capitalism in the open air: profit with purpose, business as a force for good. Every cabin booked, every coffee poured, every kid on the ropes course makes this a more vibrant village — the kind of place amazing children grow up in.
